Available Endpoints

GET /v1/market/prices

Retrieve real-time market prices for coffee commodities. Supports filtering by type and exchange.

# Example Request curl -X GET "https://api.shinaebank.com/v1/market/prices?types=arabica,robusta" \ -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY" # Example Response { "data": [ { "type": "arabica", "price": 185.50, "currency": "USD", "timestamp": "2026-06-15T14:30:00Z" }, { "type": "robusta", "price": 142.30, "currency": "USD", "timestamp": "2026-06-15T14:30:00Z" } ] }
POST /v1/trading/orders

Place a new trading order. Supports market, limit, and stop orders for all traded commodities.

# Example Request curl -X POST "https://api.shinaebank.com/v1/trading/orders" \ -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY" \ -H "Content-Type: application/json" \ -d '{ "instrument": "arabica_futures", "side": "buy", "quantity": 5.0, "order_type": "limit", "price": 185.00 }'
GET /v1/supply/warehouses

Get real-time status and capacity information for all smart warehouses in the network.

# Example Request curl -X GET "https://api.shinaebank.com/v1/supply/warehouses?region=asia_pacific" \ -H "X-API-Key: YOUR_KEY"

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading on SHINAE Coffee Bank? ▼
To start trading, you need to: (1) Create an account and complete KYC verification, (2) Fund your account via wire transfer or crypto, (3) Navigate to the Trading page, (4) Select your instrument and place a buy/sell order. Institutional accounts can also access the API for algorithmic trading.
What is aeroponic coffee and why does it matter for trading? ▼
Aeroponic coffee is grown without soil using nutrient mist, resulting in 30% higher yield and superior quality. SHINAE Coffee Bank is the first platform to tokenize and trade aeroponic coffee as a distinct commodity class, offering premium pricing and sustainable investment exposure.
How does the warehouse receipt financing work? ▼
After storing coffee in our certified warehouses, you receive a digital warehouse receipt (tokenized on blockchain). This receipt can be used as collateral to borrow up to 70% of the coffee's value at competitive rates. Loan approval typically takes less than 24 hours.
What are the trading hours? ▼
Spot trading is available 24/7 on SHINAE Coffee Bank. Futures trading follows the schedule of the underlying exchange (ICE US: Mon-Fri 04:00-14:00 EST, ICE Europe: Mon-Fri 02:00-16:30 GMT). Our Market Data Feed operates continuously.
How is the price of Yunnan Aeroponic coffee determined? ▼
Yunnan Aeroponic coffee trades at a premium to Arabica futures, typically $15-25/ton above ICE Arabica. The price is determined by: (1) ICE Arabica benchmark, (2) Aeroponic quality premium, (3) Yunnan regional supply/demand, (4) USD/CNY exchange rate. Our AI model updates the fair value price every 10 seconds.
Is my fund safe on the platform? ▼
Yes. Client funds are held in segregated accounts at Tier-1 banks. We use multi-signature cold wallet storage for crypto assets. Our platform is audited annually by Deloitte. Digital warehouse receipts are insured up to $50M per warehouse by Lloyd's of London.

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, verify your identity (KYC), place trades, or contact support. We implement industry-standard security measures including: AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit, multi-factor authentication, regular third-party security audits, and segregated hot/cold storage for digital assets. We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Trading records are retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations.
